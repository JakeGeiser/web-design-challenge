Property Cost by State

Breaking down the average property cost down by state, we see for Virginia and Maryland, starting in 2014, they both got far more expensive to live in. This is perhaps due to a change in the economy combined with theses states being near Washington D.C. The cheapest state to live in appears to be West Virginia. Further analysis on increasing/decreasing population into these states would be an enlightning next step.
